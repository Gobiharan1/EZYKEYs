Why 90 Minutes

Let us explain why we firmly believe 90-minute lessons are the best choice for most learners.
⏱️

The 90-Minute Difference

Many driving schools offer shorter lessons. At Ezy Keys, we strongly recommend 90 minutes because it gives learners more time to settle, practise, correct mistakes, and build confidence with clear guidance.

8 Reasons to Choose 90 Minutes

⏱️

More real driving time

Practise properly without feeling rushed or pressured.

🚀

Faster progress

Longer sessions help you learn and refine skills more naturally.

💪

Build confidence faster

Extra time to settle in, relax, and drive comfortably.

🔄

Master tricky skills

Roundabouts, merging, lane changes, three-point turns, parallel parking — you get enough time to practise them properly.

🎯

Get test-ready faster

Longer sessions allow deeper practice and stronger preparation for real test conditions.

💰

Better value per lesson

More driving, more personalised feedback, and clearer improvement each session.

📘

Convenient for busy schedules

One longer session often replaces two shorter ones, saving time and reducing travel.

🧠

Fewer trips needed

More efficient for learners who want to progress quickly.
